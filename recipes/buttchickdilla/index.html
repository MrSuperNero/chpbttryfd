The ButtChickDilla

5 minutes if you don't burn anything

~$3.00, but could be more if you really want to gain weight.

3/5 tablespoons of butter

A beautifully exquisite taste of nostalgia and mediocrity combined with the extremely low level of cooking technique and expertise required for this dish truly elevates it to the middle of the list of our favorite foods. The key is to cook the chicken nuggets in a pool of (at least 3 tbsps of) butter. The more, the better, but do keep your wallet in mind as you tackle this challenge. Good luck, fellow fiscally conservative high-cholesterol-enthusiasts.
