Buttered Eggs

2 minutes if you don't burn anything

~$1.50 if you get the necessities (eggs, cheese, butter)

10232/5 sticks of butter (yes, it's so good you can die instantly)

now this is the holy grail of buttered dishes. Buttered eggs are the Derrick Rose of easy-to-make buttery delicious foods. Honestly, it can't get better than this. I mean sure, it looks like the endoplasmic reticulum but it tastes like the mitochondria (cuz it's a powerhouse of food, get it? get it???) All you gotta do is melt some butter, drop some eggs in there, gently caress and massage some cheese into there, and mix that bad boi up for like 30 seconds and you're done. I mean, you gotta add salt n' pepa cuz you're not an animal (or are you?)
